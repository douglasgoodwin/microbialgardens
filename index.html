Microbial Gardens is a place where the invisible becomes visible. Engineered bacteria provide sights and smells drawing you into the latest exchanges.

Scientists estimate that microbes outnumber human cells by nearly 30%. We don’t have a good idea about what these microbes are doing and how they interact with our cells. Microbial Gardens is a living ecology that shows dynamic exchanges between microbes. Living pavilions quite literally with one another for resources. Some of the pavilions provide food and energy to others. Other pavilions prey upon the providers. This dramatic ecology is here for you to examine.
